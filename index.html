About us

The scientific conference “INTERNATIONAL SCIENCE AND INNOVATION IN THE 21st CENTURY” is an open program. The main goal of the scientific conference is to highlight the achievements and prospects of world science and education, current problems and ways to solve them, as well as the history and stages of the development of science and education in the process of modeling innovations in science and education.


The purpose of the “INTERNATIONAL SCIENCE AND INNOVATION IN THE 21st CENTURY” conference is to inform the scientific community about the research and innovations of experts and scientists in all fields, to increase the interest of the young generation in science and education, and to explain scientific concepts.
